Ëtridefi Bloc Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2, 2024

Introduction

Ëtridefi Bloc ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Ëtridefi Bloc is a non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet. We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your private keys or seed phrases
  • Your wallet addresses
  • Your transaction history
  • Your personal identification information
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts or phone data

Local Data Storage

All sensitive data, including your encrypted private keys and wallet information, is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is protected by your device's security features (PIN, biometrics, etc.).

Blockchain Data

When you make transactions, your wallet interacts directly with public blockchain networks. Blockchain transactions are public by nature and are not controlled by us.

Third-Party Services

Our app may connect to third-party services for:

  • Price data (cryptocurrency prices)
  • Blockchain RPC nodes (to broadcast transactions)
  • DEX aggregators (for token swaps)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Analytics

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve our app. This data cannot be used to identify individual users and does not include any wallet or transaction information.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Local encryption of sensitive data
  • Biometric authentication support
  • No server-side storage of private keys

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@etrid.io
  • Website: https://etrid.io

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your wallet data is entirely under your control on your device.
